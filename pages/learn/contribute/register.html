Register

Anyone who uses AusStage is welcome to register as a user.

Registered users can log in to AusStage with a username and password. On completing a program of training in data entry protocols and procedures, registered users can add, edit and copy AusStage records.

When you register with AusStage you will be asked to share your contributions under the Creative Commons licence: Attribution, Non-commercial, Share Alike. Under this licence, you grant AusStage permission to share your contributions with other users.

Users are advised that all contributions to AusStage, including comments and tags, are subject to editorial review. Please read our Terms of Use, Cultural Advice and Privacy Policy for further information. Please contact the Project Manager if you have any questions.
